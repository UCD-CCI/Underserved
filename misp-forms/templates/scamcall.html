Report Scam Phone Call

A scam call is a fraudulent phone call made with the intent to deceive the recipient and gain access to sensitive information or money. Scammers often pose as representatives from trusted organisations, such as government agencies, banks, or tech support services, to appear legitimate. These calls may involve urgent language, threats, or enticing offers to pressure or lure the recipient into compliance. Common types of scam calls include claims about unpaid taxes, compromised accounts, fake lottery winnings, or technical issues that require immediate action. The ultimate goal is to convince the recipient to share personal information, provide payment, or install malware, all of which compromise security and privacy.
