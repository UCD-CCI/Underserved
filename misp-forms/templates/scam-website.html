Scam or Suspicious Website

A scam website is a fake site designed to trick people into sharing personal information, such as passwords, credit card numbers, or other sensitive data, or to steal money by pretending to offer products, services, or opportunities. These sites often look very similar to real websites, using fake logos, names, or even slightly altered web addresses. The goal is to deceive visitors and gain their trust so they fall for the scam.

The safest and most effective way to share information about a Scam or Suspicious Website is by using Lookyloo. Lookyloo securely captures and collects all relevant data from the targeted website.


After analysis is complete, you can submit the website to MISP for sharing.

  1. On the Lookyloo page, select Actions.
  2. Click Prepare to Push to MISP.
  3. Click Push to MISP.
